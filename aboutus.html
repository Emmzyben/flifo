About Our Organisation

FLOURISHING LIFE INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATION(FLIFO)is a non-governmental organization based in NIGERIA,
founded as a Non-Profit and Non-Governmental Organizationand a member of the Organization of Women in Sciences For
Developmenting Countires(OWSDC).
FLIFO is dedicated to promoting integrated development,Health Education,Capacity Development of Young and Vulnerable population. Empowerment.
The FLIFO Organization is trying hard with the community – based efforts to improve the living condition of the deprived section of society.

The FLIFO team comprises of people from multidisciplinary and multi-sector backgrounds across NIGERIA and THE CONTINENT OF AFRICA
that share a common platform of thinking and doing their bit towards the making of a cognitive society, to intending to put collective efforts to work
together and gather people who find themselves inspired to uplift the lives of those people who grow with inadequate education, health care,
resources, and psychological support – several calls them as “underprivileged”..

FLIFO runs on a fully decentralized management system,having Mrs Nkiru Chinwe Onuko as THE FOUNDER/ EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR
who is supported by a cadre of professional and administrative staff. Program and performances are monitored on a regular basis and constant
review and planning meetings. Eminent experts from the fields of management, agriculture, environment, health, women empowerment,
social work, etc. are invited to these meetings/events.

Organisational structure

FLIFO firmly believes in multiplying its efforts through collaboration & partnerships that bring together government, non-government, and corporate in a collaborative process for socio-economic development, improving agriculture, environment, health, and women/child empowerment. FLIFO puts a lot of emphasis on upgrading the skills and capacities of its staff and Volunteers. For this purpose, need-based training is regularly organized in different areas of skills acquisitions

Our Vision

We aim to create a healthy, learned and empowered society for the Youth, Women and girls.

Our Mission

To transform the lives of Youth, Women and girl through proffering solutions for the promotion of a healthy lifestyle, STEM education, economic empowerment and the development of human capacity for sustainability.

Recommendation letter from international STEM partnering organization

To Whom It May Concern: I am pleased to write this recommendation letter for Mrs. Nkiru, founder, and executive director of Flourishing Life International Foundation in Nigeria. I have known Mrs. Nkiru for about a year and have been inspired by her unwavering commitment to promoting women's empowerment in STEM education in Nigeria. Mrs. Nkiru's work is closely aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly Gender Equality and Quality Education. Her initiative focuses on empowering women and girls in STEM education, which is crucial for achieving these SDGs. Mrs. Nkiru's initiative has had an extraordinary impact on the achievement of the SDGs in Nigeria. By collaborating with traditional rulers such as the king of her village and elders, she has engaged men in supporting women's empowerment through STEM. This has resulted in a significant increase in the participation and representation of women in STEM fields in Nigeria, which is critical for achieving gender equality and inclusive education. Mrs. Nkiru's approach is innovative and has the potential to be scaled up and replicated in other parts of Nigeria and beyond.
